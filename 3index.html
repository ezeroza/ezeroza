Asian Inspirations

Ingredients: Chicken, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers), rice.
Instructions: Stir-fry chicken with ginger and garlic. Add vegetables and soy sauce. Serve over rice.

Wok ablaze, a fragrant dance,
Chicken Stir-Fry, a spicy chance.
Ginger's zest, a soy-sauce dream,
A culinary, flowing stream.

Ingredients: Sushi rice, seaweed (nori), fish (salmon, tuna), avocado, cucumber.
Instructions: Spread rice on nori. Add fish and vegetables. Roll tightly. Slice and serve.

Rice and seaweed, a careful art,
Sushi rolls, a work of heart.
Ocean's bounty, a vibrant sight,
A taste of freshness, pure delight.

South American Flavors

Instructions: Marinate fish in lime juice. Add red onion, cilantro, and chili peppers. Serve chilled.

Ceviche sings, a joyful clang.
